Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7th 2025

This privacy policy describes how Padlock Puzzle collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

  1. Information We Collect
    • a. Personal Information
      We do not require you to provide personal information to use Padlock Puzzle. However, you may optionally provide a player name and email address for personalization and support.
    • b. Usage Data
      We may collect anonymous usage data, such as game progress, level completion, and in-game actions, to improve the app experience.
    • c. Device Information
      We may collect non-personal device information (such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers) for analytics and troubleshooting.
    • d. In-App Purchases and Ads
      Padlock Puzzle uses third-party services for in-app purchases and advertisements (such as Google Mobile Ads and Google Play Billing). These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.
  2. How We Use Information
    • To provide and improve the app experience
    • To save your game progress and settings
    • To process in-app purchases
    • To show relevant advertisements
    • To respond to support requests
  3. Third-Party Services
    We use the following third-party services, which may collect information as described in their privacy policies:
    • Google Play Services
    • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
    • In-App Purchase (Google Play Billing)
    • Games Services (Google Play Games)
    Please refer to their privacy policies for more information.
  4. Data Retention
    We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the app and for legitimate business purposes.
  5. Children's Privacy
    Padlock Puzzle is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  6. Security
    We take reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.
  7. Changes to This Policy
    We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on this page.
